THE POET JOSEPH

Poetry

the tide comes and goes
sometimes thief, sometimes giver
rising and sinking


surely Christ must have
    witnessed an evening like this
out on the sea of galilee
drifting past water fowl
    bobbing on gentle waves
gentle waves pattering
    the side of the boat

peace perfected such that
you hate to even make a ripple

perfect peace on this sea
like a sip of water in God’s palm

deer on the bank drinking
flowers on the bank living

rest


Lord, as the leaves die
the wind carries them away

stirring all their colors
like a canvas full of paint

twirling their shapes
as if they’ve come alive again

Lord, as the leaves die
make my life like that I pray

dancing in your breath
beautiful today

written by your breath
beautiful by grace


the red cardinal
so bright
makes an easy target

but who could kill
something so beautiful

it steals his pain
and flutters away
